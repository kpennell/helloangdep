About Vibesample

There’s so much good music being played any given night in Denver.

But it’s hard to find out who’s playing where and what they sound like.

Vibesample is an effort to make discovering live music really easy.

The hope/goal of doing this is that more people will go to more live music shows.

This will create more joy, dancing, face to face interaction, and help artists find new fans.

Vibesample was created by Kyle Pennell
